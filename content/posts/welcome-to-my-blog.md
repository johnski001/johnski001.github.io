Dear reader,

Welcome to the first post on my new tech-blog. This website will contain articles about my personal interests.
As these posts can be very different from each other, all of them will touch the subject *technology*.

After running a successful blog in the past it is again itching to start over. In the past my blog was located at [bloobz.be](https://web.archive.org/web/20160130155446/http://www.bloobz.be/). 2025 will be the year where I start blogging again, doing posts about **smart home**, **home servers** and **homelabs**, **product reviews** and so on. 

I will touch consumer electronics and more business oriented material as well.

I want to differ myself from other techbloggers by running a privacy respecting blog. This means that I will not implement any cookies, tracking or metrics. As you will read in future posts, digital privacy is by far the most important thing to guard on the current internet.

So welcome to my tech hub!
